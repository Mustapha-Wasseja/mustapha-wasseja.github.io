Featured Package

spmixW

Bayesian Spatial Panel Data Models with Convex Combinations of Weight Matrices

An R package for Bayesian MCMC estimation of spatial panel data models, including SAR, SDM, SEM, SDEM, and SLX specifications with fixed effects. The package supports convex combinations of multiple spatial weight matrices and Bayesian model averaging over subsets of weight matrices.
