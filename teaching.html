Approach

Teaching philosophy

I emphasize conceptual clarity, empirical application, and reproducible quantitative practice. I aim to help students understand not only how models work, but also when they are useful, what assumptions they require, and how they can inform economic and policy decisions.

I am especially interested in teaching econometrics and statistics through practical, transparent workflows that make quantitative evidence easier to understand, evaluate, and communicate.
